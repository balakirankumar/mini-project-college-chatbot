About NGIT

Neil Gogte Institute of Technology is the first engineering college to be started after the formation of Telengana state. It is started by Keshav Memorial Technical Educational Society with the sole purpose of promoting quality education from pre primary school to P.G courses.

The present management committee comprises of luminaries having vast experience in different fields from legal to education. It is a sister concern of KMIT and all the facilities and teaching learning processes will be implemented in the new college.
